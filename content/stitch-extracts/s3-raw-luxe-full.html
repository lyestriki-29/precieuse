L’Or 19KT

A Portuguese tradition. This high-purity alloy offers a unique depth of color and superior durability for heritage pieces meant to outlast time.

GVS Diamonds

We select diamonds based on character, not just metrics. Every stone is hand-picked for its fire and structural integrity within the setting.

Traceability

The Kimberly Process is our baseline. Our commitment extends to knowing the exact mine and the hands that handled the raw stones.
